I am currently a student at Noroff - School of Technology and Digital Media, where I am studying Frontend Development. I am on my last semester and will be finishing my last exam in the summer of 2021.

My passion for creativity started early when I started ballet dancing at age of 6. I quickly found out I hated stockings so that did not last long. Further on I started painting classes, then moved on to hand drawing portraits.

At the same time I loved creating neat lists on either paper or in excel. I had lists for everything and loved to simplify them over and over again.

The passion for computer grew more when reaching the age of 18. I was playing tournaments in League of Legends and raidleading World of Warcraft raids.

After graduating high school I figured out I wanted to combine my love for creativity and structure, and then found the frontend profession. The passion for structure and user friendly layout is perfect for this job, with the room to also be creative in so many ways. It gives me great joy to work like this.
